Title: Once upon a time
Folder: Poems
Created: Jun 17, 2023 at 12:51 AM
Body: 
Once upon a time 
I signed along the dotted line
Full belief in this little dream of mine
Little did I know things would be fine 
Along that dotted line, written in text oh so fine 
A deal set in stone with no lifeline 
Once upon a time 
I found someone who calls me “mine”
